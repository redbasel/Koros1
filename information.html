This is the information page for this experimental dashboard. This dashboard is part of a platform where coaches can monitor and keep updated regarding their clients.

Dashboard

The information page is up and running as well as the follow up page.The dashboard is from the clients point of view.

Follow Up

The follow up page is where the client will weekly update their coach about their improvements.

Plans

The "plans" page is where the client will be provided with downaodable files or links where they can access their workout and meal plans, this part will not be updated in MS1.

Progress

Progress page is where the client will be able to review their information and progress, this part will be enabled after MS1.
